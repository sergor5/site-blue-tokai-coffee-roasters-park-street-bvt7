More Than Just A Cafe

Located at 31 Chowringee Road, opposite the iconic Park Hotel, our Park Street outlet is a sanctuary for coffee lovers in Kolkata. We believe in transparency, traceability, and taste.

From the sourcing of Indian Arabica beans to the final pour, every step is curated to provide you with a world-class coffee experience. Whether you are here for a quick espresso or a long conversation, our ambiance sets the perfect mood.
